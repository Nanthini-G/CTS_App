Life Insurance Policy – SecureLife

1. Sum Assured:
- Minimum sum assured: ₹2,00,000
- Maximum sum assured: ₹1,00,00,000

2. Premiums:
- Premium payment options: Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly
- Grace period: 30 days for yearly, 15 days for monthly payments

3. Exclusions:
- Suicide within the first year is not covered.
- Death due to participation in hazardous sports is excluded.

4. Maturity Benefits:
- On policy maturity, the sum assured along with bonuses will be paid to the nominee.

5. Claim Process:
- Nominee must submit death certificate and policy documents.
- Claims are processed within 15 working days.
